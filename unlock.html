Unlock 14.0

UNLOCK 14.0 is an excellent initiative taken by IEEE Hyderabad Section,Student Activities Committee & Computer Society Chapter. The first Code Contest as a part of Unlock 14.O was a 6 hours coding contest that commenced on 2nd of June,2020 at 10 a.m. and ended at 4 p.m.Volunteers are the backbone of every event conducted under the hood of IEEE and the volunteers of IEEE Hyderabad Section did not fail to prove it again. Dr.G Sree Lakshmi, the Chairperson of Student Activities Committee; Mr. Vidya Sagar, the Chairperson of the IEEE CS Chapter Hyderabad Section provided their restless support in achieving a remarkable outcome for the first Code Contest and Mr. Bala Prasad Peddigari, Secretary and Mr. Amit Kumar, Chairperson IEEE Hyderabad Section have provided their major support to the initiative.

The UNLOCK 14.0 was operated on the HackerRank platform where more than 50 problem statements comprising various levels of difficulties were given as challenges. The registrations started 10 days prior to the date of the competition i.e. May 23, 2020, and ended on June 2, 2020, at 09:00 a.m. to give the students ample time for preparation. The total number of registrations clinched a mark of 2198.

1 st Coding Contest

The Section celebrated the success of the first Code Contest as a part of UNLOCK 14.0 with a remarkable number of students making good use of knowledge about the pattern of IEEEXTREME PROGRAMMING COMPETITION and their valuable time during the COVID-19 pandemic situations with the resources, activities, talks, workshops and opportunities provided by the Section through Computer Society Chapter and Student Activities Committee.

The most awaited competition began at 10:00 a.m. on June 2nd, 2020, with 1608 participants competing with each other on the leaderboard of HackerRank and actively solved problems at breakneck speed. The response of the participants was unmatched as they did not stop till 4:00 p.m. The organizers and volunteers were active for six hours to make sure there were no glitches faced by the participants and solved each and every query with a lot of patience. They ensured that the competition was fair enough by steering clear of available codes online from various coding platforms. They achieved this by performing a plagiarism check every once in a while. The volunteers also kept the participants entertained by asking them to click their pictures while coding. The best pictures were posted on the social media handles of the participants tagging the section profiles in order to create the trend required on the social media.
